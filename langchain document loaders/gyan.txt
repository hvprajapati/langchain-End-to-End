The Road of Learning

Every journey starts with a single, trembling step,
A question asked, a promise quietly kept.
We stumble first, then slowly find our way,
Turning the dark of doubt to light of day.

Knowledge is not a treasure locked in gold,
But a living flame that grows as it is told.
Each book we read, each lesson that we share,
Plants a small seed of wisdom everywhere.

Failure is not the ending of the fight,
But a teacher dressed in shadows of the night.
It shows us where our weakness quietly hides,
And points us to the strength that still abides.

So walk the road of learning, brave and slow,
For every step will help your spirit grow.
The mind that dares to wonder, seek, and try,
Will one day touch the wisdom of the sky.
